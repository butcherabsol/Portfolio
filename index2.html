Our team comprises Mike Glasek Business Lead of our project and student at GMBA, Ivan Kakhaev our Tech Lead, Ph.D. student of Computer Science and, Jacek Fleks, also a GMBA student and a team leader for this project. We joined the competition with our project of a split ergonomic keyboard, a product designed to mainly: increase portability, typing speed, and customizability of typing – a task that is too familiar for us as students.

Ergotype - Concept video

April 7 - Our primary technological advantage is split connectivity – this is a new technology. Such connectivity toany device is almost seamless, with incredible portability, programable functions and layers. With included connectivity to multiple devices, we offer an entirely wireless experience with connectivity between modules and connected devices and, for ultimate comfort - a case with adjustable height. Our recent development is a keyboard case that, in the final version, will be able to elevate the keyboard for ultimate ergonomics and snap halves together for fast and convenient travel. Elevation allows for more natural arm and wrist angles than traditional flat keyboards.
